Short Essay on Data Breaches
Link: https://securityboulevard.com/2020/06/nintendo-confirms-additional-140000-accounts-compromised-in-april-data-breach/
Link:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2020-2021_Nintendo_data_leak

In April 2020, Nintendo had a data breach effecting nearly 300,000 users. This included unauthorized logins and credit card charges to their accounts. 

In the data breach, user's nicknames, birthdates, country, region, email and gender were all stolen. This breach was done with a technique called "credential stuffing". This tecnique often means that users who use the same username and password for multiple sites may experience a large financial loss as the hacker uses this information to access many other sites.
